Our Story

More Than a Meal — It's an Experience

Turtle Jack's has been a cornerstone of casual dining in Ontario for over two decades. In Etobicoke, we bring that same warm, lively spirit to every table — whether you're gathering with family, catching a game with friends, or celebrating a special occasion.

Our kitchen is built on bold flavours, fresh ingredients, and recipes that make people come back again and again. Pair that with a full bar, attentive service, and a room that feels like home, and you have Turtle Jack's.
