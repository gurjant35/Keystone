Keystone Home Products was founded in 1994 with a simple philosophy: do whatever the job requires to get it done right. For over thirty years we have worked alongside homeowners, contractors, and builders across the GTA to deliver glass and aluminum solutions that meet every schedule, every specification, and every vision.

We understand the challenges of home building because we have lived them. That understanding is what makes us different — and why our clients return to us project after project, generation after generation.
